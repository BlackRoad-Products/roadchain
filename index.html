01 — Features
Trust Without Intermediaries
Every event in BlackRoad OS gets cryptographically stamped, anchored, and indexed. Tamper-proof by design.
E

Event Stamping

Every agent action, file change, memory write, and transaction gets a cryptographic timestamp. SHA-256 hashed and sequenced into the chain. Immutable from the moment it enters. Full provenance trail for every bit that moves through the OS.

A

Anchor Proofs

Periodic anchor blocks bind your local chain to external checkpoints. Cross-reference integrity across devices, agents, and time. Anchors are the backbone — even if a node goes offline, the proof persists. Distributed trust without a central authority.

M

Merkle Tree

Every block references its parent through a Merkle hash tree. Verify any single event without downloading the entire chain. Efficient, lightweight, and mathematically certain. One changed bit invalidates the entire branch above it.

B

Block Explorer

Browse the full chain visually. Filter by block type, agent, timestamp, or hash prefix. Drill into any block to see its payload, parent hash, and verification status. The entire history of your digital life, searchable and transparent.

02 — Chain Guardians
Four Agents. Zero Compromise.
These agents watch the chain. They verify, audit, challenge, and protect every block that enters.

G

Gematria — Pattern Analysis

Scans the chain for statistical anomalies, frequency shifts, and structural patterns. Detects irregularities before they propagate. Numbers don't lie — Gematria reads them fluently.

P

Portia — Ethical Review

Evaluates chain entries against ethical guidelines. Flags actions that violate trust boundaries or consent frameworks. The moral compass of every block.

A

Atticus — Compliance Audit

Runs continuous audit sweeps across the chain. Validates hash integrity, checks sequence order, and produces audit reports. If something is off, Atticus finds it.

V

Valeria — Security Enforcement

Guards the chain perimeter. Monitors for unauthorized writes, replay attacks, and hash collisions. Post-quantum aware. Valeria sleeps with one eye open.
